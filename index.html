Importance of statistics

This minimalist site brings together narrative articles written by Joseph Barbier on the subject of statistics and their relevance to everyday life.

The articles are intended to be non-technical and narrative, to make them more enjoyable to read. Any feedback, whether on form or content, is highly appreciated. Send me a message on LinkedIn or at joseph.barbierdarnal@gmail.com.

If you're interested in adding your own article or improving the site, you can contact me and/or add your contributions here.
